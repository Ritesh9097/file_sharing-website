File sharing is the practice of distributing or providing access to the digital media, such as computer programs, multimedia files (audio, images, and video), documents, presentations or electronic books.
File sharing may be achieved in a number of ways.Common methods of storage, transmission and dispersion include manual sharing utilizing removable media, centralized servers on computer networks, World Wide Web-based hyperlinked documents, and the use of distributed peer-to-peer networking..

File sharing is the act of sharing one or more computer files over a network with someone in the same house, a team member at work, a friend in another country.
You can also use file sharing to access your own files from anywhere. You can share files over a local network in an office or at home, or you can share files over the internet.
